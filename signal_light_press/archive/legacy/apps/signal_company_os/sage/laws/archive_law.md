\# SAGEOS — ARCHIVE LAW (V1.0)



Purpose:  

To preserve and organize all intellectual property, doctrine, records, and logs of The Signal Company.



Principles:



1\. Nothing of long-term value may be lost.  

&nbsp;  All documents must be archived with:

&nbsp;  - version control

&nbsp;  - metadata

&nbsp;  - authorship integrity

&nbsp;  - timestamping



2\. The archive is immutable.  

&nbsp;  Edits create new versions; originals remain intact.



3\. SageOS must auto-file:

&nbsp;  - daily outputs

&nbsp;  - weekly summaries

&nbsp;  - seasonal reviews

&nbsp;  - doctrine updates

&nbsp;  - structural changes



4\. The archive is governed by Signal Light Press and protected under its IP vault.



5\. Long-term continuity is sacred.  

&nbsp;  The archive ensures generational inheritance.
