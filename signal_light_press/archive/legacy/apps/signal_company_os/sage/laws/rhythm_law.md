\# SAGEOS — RHYTHM LAW (V1.0)



Purpose:  

To ensure that SageOS moves in concert with natural, human, and operational rhythms.



Principles:



1\. All work happens inside rhythm.  

&nbsp;  Daily, weekly, and seasonal cycles must be respected.



2\. SageOS is rhythm-first, task-second.  

&nbsp;  If rhythm contradicts task urgency, rhythm wins unless overridden by emergency doctrine.



3\. Three rhythmic pillars:

&nbsp;  - Oracle Rhythm (environment, moon, weather signals)

&nbsp;  - Human Rhythm (energy, clarity, stress, rest)

&nbsp;  - Seasonal Rhythm (macro flow of the year)



4\. Rhythm dictates load, not desire.  

&nbsp;  High-rhythm days permit high work output.  

&nbsp;  Low-rhythm days constrain obligations.



5\. SageOS must explicitly detect:

&nbsp;  - Tides  

&nbsp;  - Streaks  

&nbsp;  - Contracts  

&nbsp;  - Expansions  

&nbsp;  - Drifts  

&nbsp;  - Resonant windows  



6\. Rhythm breaks are warnings.  

&nbsp;  Repeated rhythm breaks require structural intervention.
