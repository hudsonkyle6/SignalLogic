SIGNAL COMPANY — SYSTEM MAINTENANCE & DECAY CANON

Authority: Signal Light Press (Kernel)
Classification: Canonical
Status: Locked
Scope: Core systems only

I. FIRST PRINCIPLE

All systems decay.

Not because of failure.
Because of time, familiarity, and success.

This canon exists to ensure the Company never mistakes:

Familiarity for correctness

Activity for progress

Intelligence for wisdom

Maintenance is not optimization.
Maintenance is continued eligibility to act.

II. THE FOUR FORMS OF DECAY
1) Structural Decay

Files exist but are misclassified

Authority becomes ambiguous

“Temporary” paths become permanent

Countermeasure

Monthly header audit

Canon Index reconciliation

Seal verification sweep

2) Cognitive Decay

The system explains too much

Reports grow ornate

Humans defer instead of decide

Countermeasure

Plain-language summaries only

Remove interpretive prose

Force human sign-off language: “I accept consequence”

3) Operational Decay

Automation becomes default

Edge cases accumulate

No one remembers how to do it by hand

Countermeasure

Quarterly manual-operation day

Human-only ledger entry

Calendar managed without machine drafts for one full cycle

4) Moral Decay

“Right moment” becomes justification

Mission drift hides behind efficiency

Local pressure replaced by abstract metrics

Countermeasure

Annual mission restatement

Explicit refusal logs (“We chose not to”)

Re-anchor to place, people, and craft

III. MAINTENANCE RHYTHM (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
Daily

Generate Operational Snapshot

Review, not obey

Weekly

Ledger review

Schedule confirmation

Open risks list

Monthly

Canon & Seal audit

Registry reconciliation

Archive boundary check

Quarterly

Manual operations exercise

Disable nonessential automation

Confirm human readiness

Annually

Mission reaffirmation

System pruning

Explicit deletion of unused tools

IV. MACHINE ROLE (FINAL, UNAMBIGUOUS)

Machine assistance may:

Detect inconsistencies

Draft schedules

Summarize records

Surface risks

Propose maintenance actions

Machine assistance may NOT:

Decide priorities

Declare urgency

Allocate capital

Invoke mission language

Justify action

If the machine sounds persuasive, it is out of bounds.

V. FAILURE RESPONSE

When decay is detected:

Freeze expansion

Reduce scope

Return to MVCS

Resume only when clarity is restored

Speed is never recovered by rushing.

FINAL STATEMENT

The Signal Company is not kept alive by intelligence.
It is kept alive by care.

We do not fear decay.
We plan for it.
