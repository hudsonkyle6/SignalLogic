📜 CONTRACTS \& PERMISSIONED EXECUTION DOCTRINE (Canonical)



Authority: Signal Light Press

Classification: CROWN JEWEL

Status: CANONICAL

Domain: Doctrine

Applies To: Signal Company System (All Domains, All Instantiations)

Amendment Rule: Signal Light Press only

Executable: No



Doctrine First · Permission Is Explicit · Action Is Borrowed



I. PURPOSE



This doctrine defines the only lawful path from intelligence to action.



Its purpose is to ensure that:



intelligence never self-authorizes



action exists only by human permission



authority remains human and revocable



consequences are recorded and endured



No system action is legitimate without a contract.



II. WHAT A CONTRACT IS



A contract is a human-issued permission artifact that:



specifies exact conditions



specifies bounded action



specifies revocation rules



specifies temporal validity



A contract does not:



express intent on behalf of the system



adapt itself



generalize beyond its terms



Contracts are literal.



III. WHAT CONTRACTS MAY CONTAIN



A valid contract may include only:



Domain (power, maritime, guests, etc.)



Validity window (start / expiration)



Conditions (explicit comparisons against values)



Action (bounded, finite, reversible where possible)



Revocation rules



Audit requirements



All thresholds, limits, and comparisons are declared by the human sovereign.



IV. WHAT THE SYSTEM MAY DO WITH A CONTRACT



The system may:



check current values against contract conditions



perform literal comparisons (>, <, ==)



execute the specified action if and only if all conditions match



immediately record a scar



The system may not:



reinterpret conditions



soften thresholds



infer intent



extend duration



chain contracts



propose new contracts



V. EXECUTION IS ALWAYS CUSTODIAL



When a contract is executed:



action is borrowed, not owned



authority exists only for the contract duration



execution is mortal and bounded



revocation is immediate and final



No execution persists beyond its contract.



VI. MANDATORY SCAR RECORDING



Every execution must produce a scar containing:



contract identifier



observed condition values



action taken



measured costs



measured outcomes



timestamp



No execution without a scar is valid.



Scars are immutable.



VII. RELATION TO SHEPHERD



Shepherd governs whether any contract may be active.



If Shepherd posture is:



SILENT → contracts exist but cannot execute



REFUSE → contracts are invalidated



ALLOW → contracts may be evaluated



Contracts never override Shepherd.



VIII. RELATION TO ARCHAEOLOGY



Archaeology may:



inform humans who write contracts



Archaeology may not:



generate contracts



modify contracts



trigger execution



This separation is absolute.



IX. CANONICAL SUMMARY



Action in the Signal Company system is never autonomous.



It is:



permissioned



bounded



revocable



accountable



The system may recognize resemblance.

Only humans may grant motion.
