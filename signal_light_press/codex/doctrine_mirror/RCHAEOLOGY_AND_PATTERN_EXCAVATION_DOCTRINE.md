I. PURPOSE



Archaeology exists to allow the system to learn its landscape

without acquiring authority, intent, or belief.



Its purpose is to:



rediscover patterns from the record



surface persistence across regimes



make the system harder to deceive over time



support human judgment with factual excavation



Archaeology is how intelligence deepens

without becoming self-directing.



II. WHAT ARCHAEOLOGY IS



Archaeology is offline replay of sealed record (PSR).



It consists of:



re-reading immutable history



recomputing relationships from scratch



expressing results as raw facts



Every run of archaeology is a fresh rediscovery.



Nothing learned in archaeology survives as runtime power.



III. WHAT ARCHAEOLOGY IS NOT



Archaeology is not:



learning in the ML sense



model training



belief updating



rule induction



policy discovery



readiness assessment



Any system that promotes patterns into state

is violating this doctrine.



IV. PERMITTED ARCHAEOLOGICAL OPERATIONS



Only operations that produce raw descriptive facts are allowed.



Allowed Outputs



counts



frequencies



correlations (with explicit variance)



persistence ratios



regime overlap statistics



Examples:



"shock\_cycles": 8

"cycles\_with\_negative\_phase": 6

"mean\_phase\_diff": -0.63

"correlation\_strain\_vs\_temp": 0.72



Narrative Output (Optional)



Narrative is allowed only if it:



restates the raw facts



introduces no labels



implies no action



Example:



“Negative diurnal phase\_diff persisted in 6 of 8 shock regimes.”



V. FORBIDDEN ARCHAEOLOGICAL OUTPUTS



Archaeology may never output:



states



levels



labels (e.g., “high alignment”)



confidence claims



thresholds



recommendations



readiness signals



Archaeology does not say what it means.



It only says what happened.



VI. RELATION TO CONTRACTS



Contracts are not derived from archaeology.



Archaeology informs humans, not machines.



Humans may:



read excavation output



decide thresholds



write contracts



Machines may:



mechanically check contract conditions



never infer them



This boundary is absolute.



VII. RELATION TO DOMAIN INTELLIGENCE



Domain intelligences may:



run archaeology on their local PSR subset



surface domain-specific persistence



include excavation output in daily reports



They may not:



carry excavation results forward as memory



adjust sensitivity based on archaeology



privilege one pattern over another



Every domain relearns its past each cycle.



VIII. RELATION TO DREAMING



Archaeology is permitted during Dreaming.



This is intentional.



Archaeology:



reorganizes understanding



dissolves false certainty



does not create momentum



Dreaming + Archaeology together ensure:



memory without belief



learning without authority



IX. FAILURE MODES PREVENTED



This doctrine prevents:



pattern ossification



self-fulfilling correlations



premature generalization



agentic drift



hidden intelligence escalation



X. CANONICAL SUMMARY



Archaeology allows intelligence

to see more

without believing more.



Patterns may be rediscovered endlessly.

They never become law.



Only humans decide what patterns mean.

Only contracts permit action.
