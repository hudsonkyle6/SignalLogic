Doctrine First · No Tasks · No Execution



Shepherd is the governor.

It does not act.

It decides whether anything may act at all—and defaults to silence.



1\. Purpose (Why Shepherd Exists)



Up to now we have:



Truth (Core)



Constraints (Course II-A)



Antifragile signals (II-B)



Phase recognition (II-C)



Interpretive law (II-D)



Silence semantics (II-E)



Shepherd exists to answer one question only:



“Is action permitted to exist in this moment?”



Not what action.

Not how action.

Only whether action is allowed at all.



2\. What Shepherd Is



A permission governor



A posture setter



A fail-closed authority



Shepherd emits posture, not instructions.



3\. What Shepherd Consumes (Read-Only)



Shepherd consumes outputs only:



Constraint envelopes (thermal, seasonal, energetic, structural, etc.)



Antifragile indices (unknowns, drift, brittleness, strain)



Oracle phase + confidence (if present)



Shepherd never ingests raw data.

Shepherd never reads interfaces.



4\. What Shepherd Emits



Shepherd emits exactly one of these:



posture:

&nbsp; - SILENT        # default, armored non-action

&nbsp; - ALLOW         # action may exist (bounded elsewhere)

&nbsp; - REFUSE        # action explicitly disallowed





Optionally:



posture\_confidence: float  # informational only





That’s it.



No task language.

No energy prescriptions.

No suggestions.



5\. Canonical Shepherd Laws (Locked)

Law 1 — Silence Is Default



If any required signal is missing, conflicting, or suppressed → SILENT.



Law 2 — Constraints Dominate



If any constraint envelope is below minimum coherence → REFUSE.



No phase can override this.



Law 3 — Antifragile Bias



High unknowns, drift, brittleness, or strain bias toward SILENT or REFUSE.



Shepherd protects recoverability first.



Law 4 — Phase Is Advisory Only



Oracle phase may inform posture only when:



constraints allow



antifragile indices are low



confidence is adequate



Phase never grants permission by itself.



Law 5 — No Escalation



Shepherd never escalates authority.

It may only:



allow bounded action to exist, or



withhold it.



6\. Relationship to Energy



Energy availability never authorizes action on its own.



Energy is:



conditional



bounded



subordinate to constraints



Shepherd explicitly prevents:



heroics



thrashing



optimism under suppression
