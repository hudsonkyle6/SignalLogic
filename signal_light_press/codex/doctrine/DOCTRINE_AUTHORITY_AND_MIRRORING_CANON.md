Authority: Signal Light Press

Scope: Company-wide

Effective Date: 2026-01-15



I. PURPOSE



This canon establishes the sole authority for doctrine within The Signal Company and defines the lawful relationship between doctrine, memory, and enforcement systems.



It exists to prevent:



doctrinal drift



authority inversion



emergent rule-creation by engines



silent corruption of boundaries over time



II. DOCTRINAL AUTHORITY



Signal Light Press is the sole author, editor, and sealer of doctrine.



Doctrine includes, but is not limited to:



authority limits



boundary conditions



silence and non-action



memory ethics



irreversibility constraints



role definitions



interpretive limits



governance principles



No other system, engine, or application may originate doctrine.



III. ROLE OF SAGE AND OTHER ENGINES



Sage OS, Oracle OS, Shepherd OS, Helmsman manifestations, and all downstream applications:



do not author doctrine



do not modify doctrine



do not reinterpret doctrine



They may only:



mirror doctrine



reference doctrine



enforce doctrine



freeze doctrine snapshots for integrity



Their doctrinal materials are derivative artifacts, not sources of truth.



IV. DOCTRINAL MIRRORS



Any doctrinal content stored outside signal\_light\_press/ is defined as a:



Doctrinal Mirror



A doctrinal mirror is:



read-only



frozen



traceable to a Signal Light Press source



invalid if it diverges from the source



Mirrors exist solely to support enforcement, auditability, and system integrity.



V. PROHIBITION ON ORIGINATING DOCTRINE



No engine, agent, model, or runtime system may:



invent new rules



infer new permissions



expand authority



relax constraints



reinterpret silence as permission



Any such behavior constitutes a canonical violation.



VI. CONFLICT RESOLUTION



If any doctrinal mirror, system behavior, or output conflicts with Signal Light Press doctrine:



Signal Light Press doctrine prevails.



The conflicting artifact is deemed non-canonical and must be frozen or removed.



VII. SEALING STATEMENT



This document formally binds all present and future systems of The Signal Company.



Doctrine is authored once.

Memory preserves it.

Engines obey it.



Authority preserved.



— Signal Light Press
