Doctrine First · Discovery Without Belief · Evidence Without Authority



I. PURPOSE



Archaeology exists to excavate truth from the record without creating authority.



Its purpose is to:



rediscover patterns without believing them



connect residue without compressing it into states



reveal landscape without implying intent



support human judgment without replacing it



Archaeology is how the system becomes more informed without becoming more powerful.



II. WHAT ARCHAEOLOGY IS



Archaeology is an offline, read-only replay of the sealed record.



It operates exclusively on:



PSR (Waves + Dark Field)



domain-local PSR subsets



sealed scars and outcomes



Archaeology:



replays what happened



recomputes relationships



surfaces persistence and correlation



It does not store conclusions.



Every run is a fresh rediscovery.



III. WHAT ARCHAEOLOGY IS NOT



Archaeology is not:



learning



training



inference



forecasting



optimization



decision support



Archaeology never:



emits states



emits posture



emits thresholds



emits recommendations



emits readiness



Any system that does is not archaeology.



IV. PERMITTED OUTPUTS



Archaeology may output facts only, including:



raw statistics (means, variances, counts)



persistence ratios (e.g., 6/8 regimes)



overlap frequencies between signals



time-aligned correlations (reported, not ranked)



Examples (permitted):



“Mean phase\_diff during shock regimes: -0.63”



“Correlation between strain and temperature: r = 0.72”



“Pattern observed in 6 of last 8 scarcities”



V. FORBIDDEN OUTPUTS



Archaeology may never output:



labels (e.g., “danger”, “stable”, “ready”)



states (e.g., “high risk”, “near lock”)



confidence claims



action implications



policy language



Archaeology cannot say:



what should be done



when to act



whether to trust a pattern



VI. RELATION TO MEMORY



Archaeology does not write memory.



It:



reads the record



recomputes relationships



discards results after reporting



No archaeological output may persist as runtime state.



If a pattern matters, it must be:



rediscovered again



under new conditions



against fresh evidence



VII. RELATION TO DOMAINS



Each domain may run archaeology on its own PSR subset.



Domain archaeology:



remains domain-bound



produces domain-local facts



carries no cross-domain authority



Cross-domain insight may only occur through human synthesis.



VIII. RELATION TO POSTURE \& ACTION



Archaeology runs before posture and outside execution.



It cannot:



influence Shepherd directly



gate contracts



pre-authorize execution



Archaeology informs humans.

Humans decide intent.

Contracts encode thresholds.

Posture gates action.



IX. FAILURE MODE PREVENTED



This doctrine prevents:



pattern → belief collapse



correlation → authority drift



intelligence masquerading as judgment



silent escalation through “insight”



Most systems fail here.



This one does not.



X. CANONICAL SUMMARY



Archaeology remembers without believing.

It discovers without deciding.

It informs without commanding.



Truth survives because authority does not attach to it.
