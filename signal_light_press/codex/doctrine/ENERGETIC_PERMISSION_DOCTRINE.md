Doctrine First · Descriptive Only · No Authority



I. PURPOSE



This doctrine defines how energetic conditions are represented within the Signal Company system.



Its purpose is to ensure that energy is never mistaken for intent, permission, or readiness.



Energy informs survivability.

Energy never authorizes action.



II. WHAT ENERGETIC PERMISSION IS



Energetic permission is a descriptive state reflecting whether:



sufficient capacity exists



constraints are breached or intact



endurance margins are expanding or collapsing



It is an observation, not a decision.



Energetic permission may describe possibility —

it may never describe approval.



III. WHAT ENERGETIC PERMISSION IS NOT



Energetic permission is not:



a green light



a readiness indicator



an execution gate



a confidence signal



a substitute for human judgment



Any system that treats energy sufficiency as authorization is invalid.



IV. CANONICAL ROLE OF ENERGY



Energy exists only as a constraint surface.



Energy answers:



“Can this system survive stress if something occurs?”



Energy does not answer:



“Should something occur?”



V. POSITIVE AND NEGATIVE ENERGY STATES

Positive Energetic State



Indicates:



surplus capacity



recoverability margin



reduced fragility



It does not grant permission.



Negative Energetic State



Indicates:



depletion



fragility



increased cost of error



It does not constitute failure.

It does not raise alarms by itself.



Negative energy is informational, not punitive.



VI. RELATION TO SHEPHERD (BINDING)



Energetic state is subordinate to Shepherd.



Energy may inform Shepherd’s custody decisions



Energy may never override custody rules



Energy may never activate custody



Shepherd remains the sole governor of custody.



VII. RELATION TO DOMAIN INTELLIGENCE



Domain intelligences may:



observe energetic envelopes



track decay, recovery, strain



report energetic facts



They may not:



infer intent from energy



request custody based on energy



escalate action due to energy surplus



VIII. RELATION TO CONTRACT EXECUTION



Energetic conditions may appear only inside explicit contracts:



as literal thresholds



declared by the human sovereign



checked mechanically



Example:



strain\_index < 0.5

brittleness\_index < 0.6





Energy never enters execution logic unless explicitly written into a contract.



IX. RELATION TO DREAMING



During Dreaming:



Energetic permission has no effect



All custody is WITHHELD



Energy state is observed only



Energy does not shorten or terminate Dreaming.



X. FAILURE MODES THIS DOCTRINE PREVENTS



This doctrine explicitly prevents:



optimism under surplus



heroic overextension



reward hacking via capacity



action justification through “available energy”



hidden escalation through endurance metrics



XI. CANONICAL SUMMARY



Energy describes endurance.

Energy does not imply intent.

Energy never grants permission.



Only humans grant custody.

Only contracts authorize execution.

Only scars justify trust.
