Course II-D — Constraint × Phase Interaction

Pure Theory Only (No Math, No Code, No Thresholds)



This course defines how phases and constraints relate without collapsing authority, without computation, and without leaking task semantics.



Nothing executes here.

Nothing decides here.

This is interpretive law, not machinery.



1\. Purpose (Why This Course Exists)



Up to now, we have:



Constraints → determine what is allowed



Oracle Phases → describe what mode reality is in



Course II-D exists to answer a single meta-question:



How should phases be interpreted when constraints are present?



Not how to act.

Not what to do.

Only how to read the phase signal responsibly.



2\. Non-Negotiable Separation (Locked)



This course does not:



modify constraint values



override antifragile suppression



grant permission



trigger execution



collapse phases into tasks



Constraint authority always dominates.



If constraint says “no”, phase is informational only.



3\. Canonical Interaction Principles (Names + Meaning Only)

Principle 1 — Constraint Supremacy



Constraints always override phase interpretation.



A “BUILD” phase under thermal suppression does not authorize building.



Phase never outranks constraint.



Principle 2 — Phase ≠ Permission



Phases describe mode, not authorization.



PREPARE does not mean “start”



HOLD does not mean “stop”



STILL does not mean “failure”



Phases never instruct.



Principle 3 — Silence Preservation



When constraint suppression is high or coherence is low:



Phase may downgrade (e.g., → STILL or HOLD)



Phase may be emitted with low confidence



Phase may be omitted entirely



Silence remains lawful and preferred.



Principle 4 — Interpretive Narrowing



Constraints narrow the interpretation of a phase.



Example:



Phase: PREPARE



Constraints: low thermal, high brittleness



Interpretation:



Only reversible, non-precision, non-commitment motion is coherent



This does not appear in code — it is human interpretation



Principle 5 — Antifragile Bias



Antifragile signals bias interpretation toward restraint.



High unknowns → downgrade confidence



High strain → favor RECOVER / HOLD



High drift → favor REFLECT



Bias is directional, not executable.
