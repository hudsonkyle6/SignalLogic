\## ENGINE CLASSIFICATION (BINDING)



This document defines a \*\*Layer 3 Application Engine\*\* (“Personal Hunter”).



Authority Source: \*\*Private Lineage Shepherd\*\* (posture-gated)  

Execution Authority: \*\*None\*\*  

Optimization Authority: \*\*Bounded / advisory only\*\*  

Learning Mode: \*\*Disabled by default; no continuous learning\*\*  

Connectivity: \*\*Offline only\*\*



Invariant:

The Personal Hunter may explore and summarize candidates, but it may never issue commands, guarantee outcomes, or bypass Shepherd gates.





I. PURPOSE



The Personal Hunter Engine exists to:



train judgment under bounded, survivable risk



explore markets without urgency or dependence



build a small, self-feeding portfolio



route gains toward personal debt reduction and resilience



preserve peace, sleep, and margin at all times



The Hunter is a scout, not a commander.

The human remains the sole decision-maker.



II. CANONICAL STARTING ENVELOPE (LOCKED)



These values are intentionally modest and may not be expanded without a full review cycle.



🔒 Starting Capital



$1,000 total



Fully discretionary



No borrowing



No margin



No impact on household stability



This amount is chosen to allow learning without emotional load.



🔒 Maximum Acceptable Loss



25% total drawdown ($250)



If this threshold is reached:



all positions are closed



the Hunter enters Dormancy



no re-entry occurs until a calm review is completed



Loss is survivable by design.



🔒 Maximum Position Size



15% per position ($150 max)



This prevents:



conviction creep



identity attachment



“big kill” psychology



No exceptions.



🔒 Optional Monthly Contribution



Up to $100/month



Only permitted if:



household obligations are fully met



no stress or urgency is present



debt plan remains on track



Contributions are optional. Pausing is not failure.



III. ALLOWED ASSET CLASSES (INITIAL PHASE)



Permitted:



broad index ETFs



dividend-paying, established companies



simple, explainable public equities



Explicitly forbidden:



leverage or margin



options or derivatives



crypto speculation



short selling



private placements



anything requiring urgency or constant attention



If it cannot be explained calmly on paper, it is excluded.



IV. REVIEW \& RHYTHM (DISCIPLINE OVER SPEED)

Canonical Review Cadence



Monthly review only



No daily or weekly checking



No reactionary decisions



Markets move daily.

Hunters move seasonally.



V. PROFIT ROUTING (DAVE RAMSEY ALIGNED)



All realized gains are routed in this order:



Personal debt reduction (primary)



Emergency buffer strengthening



Conservative reinvestment within this same envelope



Profits are not used for:



lifestyle upgrades



discretionary spending



confidence escalation



Freedom compounds faster than returns.



VI. DECAY, DORMANCY \& SHUTDOWN RULES



The Personal Hunter must enter Dormancy immediately if:



emotional urgency appears



sleep quality degrades



fixation or checking behavior increases



rules feel “inconvenient”



debt plan is compromised



losses tempt recovery behavior



Dormancy is protection, not punishment.



Old ideas expire without regret.

Success does not expand scope.



VII. SEPARATION FROM SIGNAL COMPANY (SEALED)



This Personal Hunter Engine:



shares no data



shares no logic



shares no outputs



shares no memory



with any Signal Company system.



This separation is permanent and non-negotiable.



VIII. FINAL CANONICAL STATEMENT



The Personal Hunter ranges only where loss is harmless.

It exists to build wisdom, not appetite.

When in doubt, it rests.
