\## I. PURPOSE



This canon defines the lawful relationship between:



\- Domain Intelligence (always-on learning within a domain),

\- Pattern Excavation (ARCHAEOLOGY; non-authoritative),

\- Human Sovereign interpretation and permission,

\- Contracted execution (bounded custody).



It is an interpretive key for all downstream doctrine and implementation.



\## II. DEFINITIONS (BINDING)



\### Domain Intelligence (DI)

A Domain Intelligence is a domain-local intelligence that:



\- observes continuously within its own envelope,

\- endures regimes across time without fatigue,

\- learns by replaying the record (PSR/Domain PSR subset),

\- excavates patterns and correlations as facts,

\- renews its view against change (daily envelopes + decay of irrelevants).



A Domain Intelligence is \*\*not\*\* sovereign.

It holds \*\*no authority\*\* and does not decide intent.



\### Human Sovereign

The Human Sovereign is the sole interpreter of meaning and intent.

Only the Human Sovereign may:



\- declare posture,

\- declare risk tolerance,

\- grant or revoke custody,

\- author contracts that permit action.



\### ARCHAEOLOGY (Pattern Excavation)

ARCHAEOLOGY is a post-seal process that replays PSR (or domain subsets) to produce:



\- persistence counts,

\- regime-conditioned recurrence,

\- correlations and co-variation,

\- anomaly clustering,

\- narrative scar linking,

\- legible factual summaries.



ARCHAEOLOGY outputs are \*\*descriptive artifacts only\*\*.

They may inform the Human Sovereign but may never function as permission.



\### Contract (Permission Surface)

A Contract is a human-authored, explicit, bounded permission to execute a defined action when literal conditions match.



A Contract:

\- carries thresholds and bounds declared by the Human Sovereign,

\- is time-limited (expires),

\- is revocable,

\- is domain-scoped,

\- requires mandatory scar recording upon execution.



\## III. THE TANDEM HELMSMAN MODEL (LOCKED)



The system operates as tandem helmsmanship:



\- Domain Intelligence recognizes resemblance and produces descriptive evidence.

\- The Human Sovereign recognizes intent and grants permission.

\- Execution occurs only as contracted custody.

\- Outcomes and costs are sealed as scars in the record.



This is not autonomous agency.

This is permissioned custody.



\## IV. THE INVARIANTS (NON-NEGOTIABLE)



1\. Intelligence may recognize resemblance.

&nbsp;  Only humans may recognize intent.



2\. Intelligence may excavate patterns.

&nbsp;  Only humans may grant permission.



3\. Patterns may never be compressed into authority.

&nbsp;  No readiness states, postures, or “go” signals may be emitted by intelligence.



4\. Contract matching must be literal.

&nbsp;  Execution logic performs non-believing checks only.

&nbsp;  No interpretation, no generalization, no inference.



5\. Every action must scar.

&nbsp;  Contracted execution must record cost and outcome to the PSR.



\## V. PROHIBITIONS (EXPLICIT)



The following are forbidden in live operation:



\- pattern → posture

\- pattern → readiness / permission proxy

\- intelligence-owned thresholds

\- intelligence-decided action

\- probabilistic authorization (“high confidence therefore act”)

\- persistence of runtime belief as authority



\## VI. COMPATIBILITY WITH PRIOR DOCTRINE



Prior doctrine remains preserved.

Where older documents imply programmatic postures, readiness levels, or internal permission signals, they are read as historical scaffolding unless explicitly amended to comply with this canon.



This canon is the governing interpretive key for all doctrine and code going forward.

\## VII. FINAL STATEMENT



The Signal Company allows intelligence to learn.

It forbids intelligence to self-authorize.



Action is never inferred.

Action is only permissioned.



The helmsman is custodial.

The navigator is human.



This canon is binding.
