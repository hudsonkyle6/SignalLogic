\# ORACLE — Canonical Phase Vocabulary (v1)



This document defines the only phase names the Oracle may emit.

Phases are modes of reality, not instructions.



No phase implies permission.

Silence is always valid.



---



\## PHASES



\### STILL

No coherent signal or high suppression.

Silence is fully lawful and dominant.



\### REFLECT

Low motion. Observation, digestion, memory integration.

Past-weighted, not future-seeking.



\### PREPARE

Energy present but constrained.

Reversible, non-committal groundwork only.



\### TEND

Maintenance of existing structures.

Care, repair, continuation — not expansion.



\### BUILD

High coherence across signals.

Irreversible construction or commitment is viable.



\### HARVEST

Realization or drawdown of what already exists.

No new commitments initiated.



\### HOLD

Energy available but intentionally withheld due to constraint conflict.

Active restraint under tension.



\### RECOVER

Strain or depletion dominant.

Restoration and recuperation required.



\### TRANSITION

Signals crossing boundaries.

Instability or ambiguity — no commitments allowed.



---



\## RULES



\- Oracle may emit one phase only.

\- Oracle may emit no phase.

\- Phase names do not imply permission or action.

\- Vocabulary changes require a new seal.
