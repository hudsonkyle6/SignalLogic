Doctrine First · One Loop · No Escape



I. PURPOSE



This doctrine defines the only lawful operational loop of the Signal Company system.



Its purpose is to:



eliminate hidden authority paths



prevent lateral control



ensure intelligence remains mortal



bind action to consequence



There is one loop.

Everything else is illusion.



II. THE SINGLE CANONICAL LOOP (INVARIANT)



The Signal Company operates exclusively through:



Observe

→ Record

→ Dream

→ Interpret

→ Contract

→ Execute

→ Scar

→ (return to Observe)





No step may be skipped.

No step may be reordered.

No step may be collapsed into another.



Any operation not traceable to this loop is invalid.



III. ROLE OF SYSTEM POSTURE



System posture defines whether execution may exist, not what execution occurs.



Posture is a global condition, not a decision.



Posture does not select actions.

Posture does not prioritize tasks.

Posture does not infer intent.



IV. CANONICAL POSTURES (LOCKED)



The system recognizes exactly three postures:



1\. WITNESS (DEFAULT)



Observe only



Record only



No contracts may execute



Intelligence may excavate patterns



Custody is WITHHELD



Silence is complete and armored.



2\. MAINTAIN



Only explicitly bounded maintenance contracts may exist



No new intent may be introduced



Actions must be reversible



Custody is narrowly ACTIVE



This posture preserves survivability, not ambition.



3\. PURSUIT



Only explicitly declared contracts may execute



Scope, duration, and revocation are mandatory



Custody is ACTIVE but fragile



This posture exists only by human declaration.



V. POSTURE AUTHORITY (ABSOLUTE)



Only the Human Sovereign may:



declare posture



change posture



authorize exit from WITNESS



No intelligence may:



infer posture



recommend posture



transition posture



Posture is intent.

Intent is human.



VI. RELATION TO SHEPHERD



Shepherd does not set posture.



Shepherd governs custody within posture.



Even in PURSUIT:



Shepherd may revoke custody



Execution must halt



Scars must still be recorded



Posture enables possibility.

Custody governs permission.



VII. RELATION TO DREAMING



Dreaming is a mandatory collapse within the loop.



During Dreaming:



Posture implicitly returns to WITNESS



Custody is WITHHELD



No execution may occur



Dreaming is not optional.

Dreaming is how the loop remains honest.



VIII. RELATION TO DOMAIN INTELLIGENCE



Domain intelligences exist inside the loop, never outside it.



They may:



observe continuously



excavate patterns



report descriptive state



match contract conditions



They may not:



bypass posture



bypass custody



initiate execution



persist momentum



They are always subordinate to the loop.



IX. FAILURE MODES PREVENTED



This doctrine prevents:



multi-loop authority



agentic autonomy



silent escalation



“always-on execution”



intelligence escaping consequence



X. CANONICAL SUMMARY



There is one loop.



Intelligence may observe and learn.

Humans alone declare intent.

Machines execute literally.

Every action scars the record.

Anything else is drift.
