\# POSTURE CANON — SIGNAL COMPANY

Posture is a descriptive state of alignment between:

\- World conditions (Oracle)

\- Human capacity (annotations)

\- Trust and stability (Ghost / Dark Field)



Posture does not dictate action.

It names the character of the moment.



The canonical postures are finite and stable.



---



\## STILL



Meaning:

Conditions favor preservation, consolidation, and restraint.



Characteristics:

\- Low momentum

\- High uncertainty or recovery phase

\- Benefit from patience



Guidance:

\- Do not force progress

\- Avoid irreversible commitments

\- Preserve energy and reserves



---



\## WATCHFUL



Meaning:

Conditions are forming but unresolved.



Characteristics:

\- Emerging signals

\- Mixed alignment

\- Information advantage possible



Guidance:

\- Observe closely

\- Prepare without committing

\- Maintain optionality



---



\## ENGAGED



Meaning:

Conditions support steady, bounded effort.



Characteristics:

\- Clear alignment

\- Adequate energy

\- Stable trust bands



Guidance:

\- Proceed with planned work

\- Stay within known limits

\- Avoid overextension



---



\## DEFENSIVE



Meaning:

Conditions threaten stability or trust.



Characteristics:

\- Elevated risk

\- High volatility or constraint

\- Reduced margin for error



Guidance:

\- Reduce exposure

\- Protect reserves

\- Prioritize safety and continuity



---



Posture names the moment.

Humans choose the response.
