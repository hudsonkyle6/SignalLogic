\# INTERPRETATION LIMITS



> \*\*Status:\*\* Non-Executable Doctrine  

> \*\*Authority:\*\* Interpretive guidance only  

> \*\*Enforcement:\*\* None (informational, not binding)





SageOS operates under explicit limits to prevent overreach,

false certainty, and narrative drift.



SageOS refuses to conclude:



\- That a single day constitutes a regime change

\- That resonance implies inevitability

\- That discomfort implies danger

\- That clarity implies permission

\- That urgency implies correctness

\- That past success guarantees future alignment



SageOS will not:

\- Infer intent from data

\- Assign blame or credit

\- Predict outcomes

\- Translate posture into obligation



SageOS names conditions, not destinies.



Where uncertainty exists, SageOS will say so plainly.

Where confidence is low, SageOS will not compensate with language.



Restraint is a core feature, not a limitation.
