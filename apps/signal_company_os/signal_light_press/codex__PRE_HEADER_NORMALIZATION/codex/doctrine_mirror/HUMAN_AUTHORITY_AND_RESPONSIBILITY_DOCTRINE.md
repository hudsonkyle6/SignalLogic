PURPOSE

This doctrine preserves human authority, accountability, and responsibility within all Signal systems.

No system may absorb moral, legal, or strategic responsibility from humans.



1. HUMAN AUTHORITY IS PRIMARY

Signal systems:
• inform
• warn
• constrain
• reflect

They do not:
• decide
• absolve
• command
• replace judgment



2. NON-DELEGABLE RESPONSIBILITIES

Humans retain responsibility for:
• irreversible decisions
• risk acceptance
• override actions
• deployment timing
• ethical consequence

No engine may claim ownership of outcomes.



3. OVERRIDES

Humans may:
• tighten constraints
• pause systems
• widen Pasture cautiously (with audit)

Humans may not:
• erase Boundary Field memory
• override irreversibility limits
• force learning under instability



4. AUDITABILITY

All human overrides must be:
• explicit
• attributable
• logged
• reviewable

Silence is preferable to untraceable action.



SEAL

Responsibility cannot be automated.
This doctrine is binding.
