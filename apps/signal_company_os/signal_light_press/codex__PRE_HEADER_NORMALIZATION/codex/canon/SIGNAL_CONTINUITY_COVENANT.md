THE CONTINUITY COVENANT

(Constitutional Law of Change)

Status: Ratified
Scope: Binding across all pillars, engines, and domains
Purpose: To constrain power, prevent self-poisoning, and preserve non-destructive continuity across discontinuous time

PREAMBLE

This covenant is not a vision statement, philosophy, or aspiration.

It is law derived from failure analysis.

It exists to prevent the system from destroying itself in pursuit of relevance, renewal, or vitality, while still allowing genuine transmission of meaning when reality has already produced a superior path.

The system exists as a lineage, not a machine.

GOVERNING LAW

Preserve by default.

Detect before destroying.

When detection is impossible without exposure, allow provisional entanglement under strict constraint.

Destroy only in service of what has proven itself.

This sequence is mandatory.
No step may be skipped, reversed, or implied.

DEFINITIONS
1. Preservation

The default posture of the system.
Preservation is not stagnation; it is stored intelligence.

All core structures persist unless lawfully removed under this covenant.

2. Detection

The identification of a proven successor pattern.

A successor pattern must:

exist in embodied form

operate under comparable constraints

demonstrate superior coherence, function, or doctrinal alignment

persist without special protection

Potential, prediction, novelty, or urgency do not qualify.

3. Provisional Entanglement

A limited, time-bound exposure undertaken only when detection cannot occur through observation alone.

Provisional entanglement must be:

Peripheral (never core-first)

Rhythm-bound (fixed duration; no extensions by enthusiasm)

Reversible (no irreversible loss)

Doctrinally constrained (meaning governs execution)

No destruction is permitted during provisional entanglement.

4. Destruction

The targeted removal of an existing structure that obstructs a proven successor.

Destruction must be:

modular

partial

regretful

memorialized

Destruction without a proven successor is prohibited.

PROHIBITIONS

The following are explicitly forbidden:

Scheduled or ritualized destruction

Destruction justified by fear, silence, decline, or perceived irrelevance

Core-first exposure or experimentation

Renewal driven by urgency, fashion, or external pressure

Erasure of destroyed structures without archival memorialization

Fear is not evidence.
Urgency is not authority.

AUTHORITY AND VETO

No action that destroys or weakens an existing structure is valid unless:

a proven successor has been documented, or

provisional entanglement has been lawfully authorized under this covenant

Any steward may invoke this covenant as an absolute veto.

No override mechanism exists.

AMENDMENT

This covenant may be amended only if all conditions are met:

Unanimous agreement of a designated steward council

Completion of a full rhythm cycle of observation

Written justification grounded in documented failure

Explicit statement of what new failure the amendment prevents

Amendments are additive and rare.

FINAL CLAUSE

The system is not obligated to change.

It is obligated not to destroy itself without evidence.

If no successor ever appears, continuity remains lawful.

This covenant prefers honest preservation over poisoned renewal.
