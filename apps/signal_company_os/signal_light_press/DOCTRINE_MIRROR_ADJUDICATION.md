## I. PURPOSE

This document formally adjudicates the contents of the
`codex/doctrine_mirror/` directory.

- preserve enforcement integrity
- provide an auditable basis for file relocation and header normalization

This record does **not** modify doctrine.
It classifies it.

---

## II. DEFINITIONS

### Doctrinal Mirror
A read-only enforcement copy of a canonical doctrine whose source
authority is Signal Light Press.

Mirrors:
- MUST contain a Doctrinal Mirror Notice
- MUST cite an exact Canonical Source
- MUST NOT introduce interpretation or new law

### Canonical Doctrine
Original binding law authored and sealed by Signal Light Press.

Canon:
- MUST live in `codex/doctrine/` or `codex/canon/`
- MUST NOT reference an upstream canonical source
- MAY be mirrored elsewhere for enforcement

### Interpretive / Reference Material
Human-facing explanation, orientation, policy, or reference.

Interpretive material:
- Is non-binding
- Must not enforce posture
- Must not masquerade as doctrine or mirror
