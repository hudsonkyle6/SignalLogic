\## DREAMING ENTRY \& EXIT (BINDING)



\### Entry Conditions (Any May Trigger)

Shepherd SHALL enter Dreaming when one or more of the following are true:

\- Recovery buffers fall below minimum threshold

\- Drift rises without corresponding external pressure

\- A consolidation phase completes

\- A seasonal or operational cycle closes

\- Success intensity exceeds stability margins

\- Narrative pressure rises faster than capacity



\### Exit Conditions (All Must Be Met)

Shepherd MAY exit Dreaming only when:

\- Recovery buffers are restored

\- Drift is stable or declining

\- Afterglow intensity has dampened

\- Identity confidence is stable

\- No urgency signals are present



\### Enforcement

\- Dreaming duration is unbounded

\- Shepherd may extend Dreaming indefinitely

\- No subsystem may override Dreaming
