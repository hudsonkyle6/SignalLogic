\# SHEPHERD POSTURE CANON



Authority: Signal Light Press  

Engine: Shepherd OS  

Classification: Protected Internal  

Status: Canonical  

Enforcement: Binding



────────────────────────────────────────

SEAL

Posture governs what may proceed.

────────────────────────────────────────



\## HOLD (Default)



Meaning:

• No expansion

• No acceleration

• No learning



Used when:

• initializing

• boundary margins unknown

• drift elevated

• buffers unverified



This is the safest posture.



\## ALLOW



Meaning:

• Limited action permitted

• Strict pacing

• Continuous monitoring



Used only when:

• buffers exceed minimums

• recovery proven

• boundary margins thick



ALLOW never removes limits.



\## RESTRICT



Meaning:

• Retraction

• De-risking

• Conservation



Used when:

• drift rising

• volatility spiking

• recovery lagging



RESTRICT may override ALLOW instantly.



\## Posture Transitions



HOLD → ALLOW  

Requires:

• multiple confirmations

• time continuity

• buffer surplus



ALLOW → RESTRICT  

May occur instantly.



RESTRICT → HOLD  

Requires stabilization.



No posture change may be forced by optimization.
