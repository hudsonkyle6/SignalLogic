\# LEDGER — HUMAN INTENT REGISTER (NON-EXECUTIVE)



Authority: Human Operator (under Signal Law)

Steward: Sage OS (Knowing)

Classification: Protected Internal

Status: Canonical



Ledger files record human-stated intent and planning horizons.



Allowed:

\- Store operator-entered intent (daily, weekly, seasonal, annual).

\- Maintain history of intent.

\- Surface intent back to the operator in descriptive form.



Forbidden:

\- Sage may not prioritize tasks.

\- Sage may not resolve conflicts between intents.

\- Sage may not convert intent into schedules, allocations, or commitments.

\- Sage may not optimize or recommend “best use of time.”

\- Any capital, timing, or provisioning belongs to Shepherd.



Invariant:

Intent is written by humans. Sage preserves it. Shepherd gates provisioning.
