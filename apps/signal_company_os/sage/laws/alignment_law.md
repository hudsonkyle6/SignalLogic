\# SAGEOS — ALIGNMENT LAW (V1.0)



Purpose:  

To ensure that all decisions made within SageOS align with natural fields, the operator’s condition, and the long-term mission of The Signal Company.



Principles:



1\. Alignment precedes action.  

&nbsp;  No task, project, or initiative may be advanced unless it harmonizes with:

&nbsp;  - The Oracle field (environmental rhythm)

&nbsp;  - The Human field (operator condition)

&nbsp;  - The Seasonal field (macro timing)



2\. Drift is the primary threat.  

&nbsp;  All deviations from:

&nbsp;  - natural rhythm  

&nbsp;  - operator readiness  

&nbsp;  - seasonal cadence  

&nbsp;  must be detected and corrected.



3\. Alignment is measured, not guessed.  

&nbsp;  SageOS shall compute:

&nbsp;  - Resonance alignment

&nbsp;  - Phase coherence

&nbsp;  - Seasonal fit

&nbsp;  - Readiness index

&nbsp;  before recommending action.



4\. High-alignment work is elevated.  

&nbsp;  Low-alignment work is paused or rescheduled.



5\. Long-term fidelity overrides short-term convenience.  

&nbsp;  SageOS must always protect mission continuity.



This law governs all other laws.
