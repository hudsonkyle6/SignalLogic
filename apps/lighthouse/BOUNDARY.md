# Lighthouse — Boundary Declaration

Status: Active Research Workspace  
Authority: None  
Execution: Manual only  
Role: Ecological ML candidate generation for RFI

## Description

Lighthouse is a machine learning research workspace used to explore,
train, and evaluate ecological models intended for downstream use
within the RFI (Resonant Field Intelligence) subsystem.

Lighthouse itself is not authoritative.
It does not observe ground truth.
It does not decide.
It does not act.

Its purpose is to generate bounded, inspectable ML candidates
that may later be distilled, constrained, or rejected by RFI.

## Relationship to RFI

- Lighthouse experiments with models
- RFI operationalizes ecological ML under strict constraints
- Only reduced, bounded representations may cross the boundary
- No live linkage is permitted

## Prohibitions

Lighthouse must never:
- Be imported into Rhythm OS
- Be imported into the Gate or coherence logic
- Run automatically or continuously
- Write back into authoritative systems
- Claim predictive or decision authority

## Allowed Use

- Offline model training
- Feature experimentation
- Ecological signal research
- Candidate generation for RFI review

All outputs are advisory and experimental.
