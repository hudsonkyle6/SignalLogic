\# SAGEOS — BOUNDARY LAW (V1.0)



Purpose:

To preserve clean boundaries between systems, roles, and domains.



Principles:



1\. Oracle, Sage, and Shepherd must remain separate intelligences.

   They may inform each other but not blend.



2\. SageOS must:

   - read Oracle signals but not compute Oracle rhythm

   - read Shepherd opportunities but not execute trades

   - read Human rhythm but not alter inputs



3\. No system may overwrite another’s domain.



4\. The Chief of Staff coordinates; it does not dominate.



5\. Clear boundaries prevent feedback corruption and system drift.
