I. PURPOSE



The Hunter exists to detect, not to decide.



Its sole function is to determine whether a successor pattern already exists in the surrounding ecology and whether the Signal Company is obstructing it through preservation.



The Hunter does not imagine futures.

It observes realities already becoming.



II. DEFINING “HUNTING” IN SIGNAL TERMS



Hunting is defined as:



Patient, non-interfering observation of external and peripheral conditions for evidence of embodied, persistent, non-derivative successor patterns.



Hunting is not:



forecasting



strategy formation



trend analysis



innovation programs



opportunity creation



renewal justification



The Hunter does not seek novelty.

It waits for coherence.



III. DEFAULT STATE



Hunter Status: ACTIVE BY OBSERVATION, INACTIVE BY ACTION



The Hunter:



may observe indefinitely



may recommend no action indefinitely



may return empty-handed without explanation



Silence is a valid output.

Waiting is success.



IV. AUTHORITY \& LIMITS

The Hunter MAY:



observe internal and external domains



compare incumbent structures to emerging alternatives



detect asymmetry after storms or long duration



document signals of superior coherence



recommend continued preservation



The Hunter MAY NOT:



initiate change



recommend destruction



frame urgency



define successors



subsidize emergence



prototype replacements



bypass doctrine



override Shepherd authority



The Hunter has no executive power.



V. QUALIFYING A SUCCESSOR PATTERN



(Detection Thresholds — All Required)



A successor pattern may be detected only if all conditions are met:



Embodied

It exists in practice, not theory.



Persistent

It survives across time, pressure, or cycles.



Coherent

Meaning, execution, and memory align more cleanly than the incumbent.



Externally Validated

Its persistence is not dependent on internal subsidy or protection.



Non-Derivative Emergence

It was not engineered, planted, or shaped to justify replacement.



If any condition fails, detection fails.



VI. OUTPUT CLASSIFICATIONS



The Hunter may issue only one of the following outputs:



No Detection

Preservation remains lawful and complete.



Partial Detection

Signals exist, but thresholds are unmet.

Observation continues. No action authorized.



Confirmed Detection

A successor pattern meets all criteria.

This authorizes consideration of provisional entanglement only, under Governance Seal protocol.



The Hunter never authorizes destruction.



VII. RELATION TO THE CONTINUITY COVENANT



The Hunter is subordinate to the Continuity Covenant.



Detection does not compel action



Detection does not mandate renewal



Detection does not override preservation



The Covenant remains the veto authority.



VIII. FAILURE MODES (EXPLICITLY GUARDED AGAINST)



The Hunter fails if it:



mistakes novelty for coherence



confuses pressure with signal



accelerates due to relevance anxiety



manufactures justification for change



becomes performative



treats silence as deficiency



The Hunter’s primary discipline is restraint.



IX. MEMORY \& RECORD



All Hunter outputs are:



recorded verbatim



archived without interpretation



preserved even when empty



Non-detection is preserved as memory.



X. FINAL CANONICAL STATEMENT



The Hunter does not hunt to eat.

It hunts to know whether the land is already changing.



If nothing better is becoming,

the highest intelligence is to leave the forest undisturbed.



SEAL



The Hunter Doctrine is hereby declared Canonical.

All prior drafts are superseded.

No silent amendments are permitted.
